【FEATURES】

- Comprehensive navigation data displays.

- Storage for up to 999 waypoints and 30 routes.

- 7 inch, color LCD, touch screen operation with adjustable brilliance.

- Dedicate port for BAM/INS connection.

- 3 GNSS data outputs.

- A DGPS beacon unit (NDG-100) may be connected to the NGR-1000/NGR-3000 to add DGPS function.

- Meets the requirements of relative IMO and IEC regulation & standards, including IMO MSC112 (73), IMO MSC302 (87), IEC61108-1, IEC 62923-1, IEC 62923-2, etc.

- DNV type approval.

- CCS type approval.

- RS type approval.

- VR type approval.
