【FEATURES】

- Designed to use for emergency purpose on lifecraft.

- AIS-SART has advantage in some aspects, such as farther-reach transmission and less affect from sea conditions.

- Designed to transmit a serial of messages on two AIS channels alternately.

- NAS-1000 adopts low consumption devices to ensure the product to continuously operate more than 96 hours.

- NAS-1000 conforms to the latest rules including IMO MSC 246（83）and IEC 61097-14.

- CCS type approval.

- DNV type approval.

- RS type approval.

- VR type approval.
