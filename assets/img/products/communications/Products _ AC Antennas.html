GNSS is a Global Navigation Satellite System for both marine and landbased solutions. GNSS covers five satellite systems in one antenna: GPS, Galileo, Glonass, BeiDou, and Egnos, all with outstanding RF specifications:
A) GPS (L1): Fc 1575.42 (+/-15: 1560-1590), VSWR:<2.0:1, 26dBd.
B) Galileo (E1): Fc 1575.42 (+/-12.276: 1563.144-1587.696), VSWR:<2.0:1, 26dBd.
C) Glonass (G1): Fc N/A (1598.0625-1605.37), VSWR:<2.0:1, 26dBd.
D) Beidou (B1): Fc 1575.42 (+/-16.368: 1559.052-1591.788), VSWR:<2.5:1, 25dBd.
E) Egnos (L1): Fc 1575.42 (+/-1.023: 1574.397-1576.443), VSWR:<2.0:1, 26dBd.
The GNSS antenna can be installed directly on the deck, it withstands walking pressure, or can be mounted on a rail mount. The antenna withstands harsh environmental conditions, both on sea and land. DOWNLOADS
